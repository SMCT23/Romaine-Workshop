• Builder fees range from 12% to 20% depending on project type and complexity

• Some line items are allowances — treat as variable inputs in estimates

• Costs reflect Central Coast (SLO County) market conditions

• Finish level is the primary cost driver — site conditions significantly impact total cost

• Normalize costs by CSI division for cross-project comparison
